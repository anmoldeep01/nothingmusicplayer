## Make Repository Public

1. Go to: https://bitbucket.org/demo1155/musicplayer/admin/access
2. Select "This is a public repository"
3. Click "Save"

Your repository will be visible at:
**https://bitbucket.org/demo1155/musicplayer**

And will appear on your profile:
